I used some animated .GIFs... I know they take longer to load and that my Lynx visitors will not be able to see them...however I do enjoy them. It started when I visited fellow ASCII artist, Shanaka Dias' homepage and saw a couple that I really liked. Turns out, he made them using GIF Construction by Alchemy Mindworkshop. He gratiously allowed me to use his email dog and his animated bubbles (aquatic creatures). Thank you Shanaka!
The animated GIF located on the Disney page was made by another ASCII artist- Allen Mullen. Thank you Allen for letting me use Mickey!

The rest of the animated GIFs I found on the internet. They are all of free use- provided that I link back to the source. So, here the sources:
