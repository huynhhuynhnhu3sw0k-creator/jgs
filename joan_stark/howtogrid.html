GIVE THE ASCII ART A TRY...
This will work with Internet Explorer 4.0+ only.
The text and symbol characters in the blue box can be dragged--
which means that if you click and hold your mouse on them,
you can move the characters to any place on the page.
You can build your own ASCII art image!
.:*~*:._.:*~*:._.:*~*:._.:*~*:._.:*~*:._.:*~*:._.:*~*:._.:*~*:._.:*~*:.
Try to build the ASCII art flower by dragging the
movable characters into the grid --one character per space.
(I've included some extra characters for you too!)

And just for fun, you can drag the butterflies to hover around the flowers!
