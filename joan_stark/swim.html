        It seems I have always loved the water.  I started swimming
  competitively as a youngster.  I swam at the age group, high school,
  and college levels.  After a dozen or so years out of the pool, I have
  returned to the world of Masters Swimming.  I swim for the O*H*I*O
  Masters team in the Lake Erie LMSC.  If you are interested in knowing
  more about U.S. Masters Swimming, let me know.  Or you can link directly
  to the USMS Web Site for locations, teams, & more information...
  See USMS Top Ten Times- I qualified for '96- S.C.M. events. (fly, back,
  IM, and relay).  I also qualified for '97- S.C.M. events (back and fly)
As of 3/98, I have started a Masters' Swimming website which includes
team info and articles that I have written for our newsletter.
